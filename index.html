Arise and Be Healed is fighting and praying everyday to be Inspirational,
Hopeful and Educational to everyone that is suffering from health issues.

This ministry is all about helping people!

What started my journey into helping others was when my father passed of a major heart attack. One minute I was talking to him. The next, he was gone from my life forever. A few years after my father passed, I watched my sister suffer within the last 3 months of her life. At that time, I was only able to help her with my thoughts and prayers. My sister died from liver damage due to being given wrong medications for her condition. After the death of my father and sister, I was broken-hearted.

Afterwards, I studied, weeks at a time, on different types of sickness and their causes. When people experience trauma and severe stress, my hope is bringing love, hope, peace, and strength in the name of Jesus. I understand that every person is in need of help. It's not about me but about helping someone else. Here at Arise and Be Healed, we share Love, Hope, and Faith to people not only with health conditions but with people who want to have healthier life choices. All I have is the wisdom of GOD, the LOVE and PASSION for the people. To enjoy each moment, take one day at a time, and the hardships as the pathway to peace and health with love for all. My Prayer is LORD grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change; courage to change the things I can; and the wisdom to know the difference.

In memory of my sister, my father love always --Amen
